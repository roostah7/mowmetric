FAQ

Is this price guaranteed?

No — it's an independent benchmark. Any quote within roughly ±15% is reasonable. Well above or below is worth questioning.

How accurate is the satellite measurement?

Within ~2% on a typical lawn, if you carefully trace just the mowable grass and skip the driveway, beds, and mulch.

What happens if I get matched with a pro?

We share your job details with up to 3 vetted local providers. They contact you with firm quotes. Pick whoever you want — or nobody. No obligation.

What do you do with my data?

Using the calculator: nothing identifiable is stored. Submitting the match form: we share your contact details with up to 3 providers — that's how we make money. We never sell your data to brokers or ad networks. See our Privacy Policy.

Why is a one-time cut more than recurring service?

Taller grass, solo trip, no route-stacking. Regular customers get about 15% off for predictable, stackable jobs.
